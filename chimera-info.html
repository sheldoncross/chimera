The Problem: The AI Industry is Hitting a Data Wall

1

Data Scarcity is Imminent

High-quality training data is a finite resource. Projections show that the supply of useful, public data will be exhausted for training large models within the next few years.

2

Quality is Degrading

The web is becoming increasingly polluted with low-quality, repetitive AI-generated content, making it harder to find pristine data for training.

3

Model Collapse is a Major Risk

Training AI on purely AI-generated data leads to a feedback loop where models lose diversity, amplify biases, and ultimately degrade in quality.
